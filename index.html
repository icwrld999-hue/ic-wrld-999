THE GENESIS

IC WRLD 999 represents the intersection of raw vulnerability and relentless ambition. Blending the infectious rhythms of Afrobeat with the introspective darkness of emo-trap, he creates a soundscape for the outliers.

From the emotional depths of "Lonely Road" to the high-energy frequency of "Go Far", IC WRLD 999 isn't just releasing music he is building a universe. Muhammed Bello known as (ic wrld 999) is a Nigerian creative and undergraduate student of Public Administration at Obafemi Awolowo University. Originally from Oyo State, he is steadily building a path that blends academic discipline with musical expression. Alongside his university studies, he has developed a strong passion for music, particularly within the Afrobeat space.He focuses heavily on emotional delivery and relatable themes. His tracks like Money Matter, Hustlers Anthem,Go far ft. DML and Getting Paid ft Mèsan reflects his interest in motivational and heartfelt storytelling through sound.
